SPACE INVADERS

A recreation of the classic game of Space Invaders using HTML,CSS and JavaScript, following the MVC design pattern and incorporating what I know of Object Oriented Programming Principles.

GOAL: To survive and shoot down all the enemy invaders before they reach the left side of the screen. The game also ends when you have either destroyed all the invaders or you have lost all your lives.
